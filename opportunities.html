Research opportunities

Full-time and part-time research assistant or research fellow opportunities based in Hong Kong.

The opportunity

Contribute to active research

I welcome inquiries from candidates interested in academic research in econometrics, statistics, machine learning, finance, and related quantitative fields.

Successful applicants contribute to ongoing projects, empirical and computational work, and the dissemination of research at professional conferences. Authorship follows scholarly contribution and standard academic practice.

Location
Hong Kong
Format
Full-time or part-time
Roles
Research assistant or research fellow

Candidate profile

What I look for

  • Bachelor’s or postgraduate training in economics, finance, statistics, computer science, or a related field.
  • Coursework in quantitative methods.
  • Proficiency in at least one major statistical programming or analysis environment.
  • Research interests or technical expertise that align with active projects.

How to apply

Start with a concise introduction.

Please send a personal statement emphasizing relevant research experience and programming skills, together with a writing sample.
