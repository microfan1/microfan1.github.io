Research agenda

Methodology and applied study

I work across econometric theory and applied research, with a particular interest in reliable inference when datasets are large, complex, or high-dimensional.

01

Econometric theory & causal inference

Identification, estimation, and inference for treatment effects, instrumental variables, and nonlinear models with high-dimensional covariates.

02

Financial econometrics & portfolios

High-dimensional portfolio construction, robust risk analysis, factor models, and machine-learning methods for investment decisions.

03

Applied econometrics

Empirical projects in labor economics, financial economics, management science, and decision-making in data-rich environments.
